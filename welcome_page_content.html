Key Features
Overview dashboard
The best place to start looking at your data straight away is the Overview Dashboard, this can be found under the reporting tab in the Dashboards button. Use this dashboard to see top level KPI performance on your account. Investigate the detail of each of your traffic sources by clicking the pie chart and check your performance over time using the period variance card.
Standard reports
Don't know what you want to see yet? Take a look at some of our standard reports to look at things like paid search performance, traffic source type performance and drill down into the detail with traffic source performance. Use these as a starting point to build up your true reporting needs.
Customising reports
You can customise any of the Standard Reports by selecting 'Additional Columns and Sorting', which is located below the date selection on each report. This will display all of the additional groupings and measures that are available to add to your report. You can also filter the results by expanding the 'Report Filters' panel on the left-hand side of the screen and selecting 'Add Filter'.
New reports
If you want something more custom select 'New' from the toolbar and choose a report type (dateview) you want to run, Equity share is good for user journey detail, landing performance for looking at PPC and other landing data, Page detail for looking at how users behave within your site and sale/action for looking into sale detail.
There are many other report types to investigate, select advanced Dataviews to look additional views of your data.
